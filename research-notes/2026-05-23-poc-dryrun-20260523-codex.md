## What Was Learned

The run found duplicate driver-name groups, but none were safe deletion candidates.
The public research rows showed that system, Bluetooth, Intel display audio, and
Lenovo/Realtek audio packages need OEM/model/device-generation evidence before any
delete decision.

## Special Notes

The Lenovo ThinkPad context is important. ThinkPad audio, Intel system, ACPI, hotkey,
and extension drivers may be OEM-customized. A generic newer driver package is not
enough evidence to delete an older local package.
